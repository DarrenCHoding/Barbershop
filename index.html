The Club

Not a salon.
A craft room.

MONO was founded on a simple idea: strip away everything that doesn’t matter and perfect what does. No loud music, no rushed chairs, no “next, please”. Just steel, leather, hot towels — and barbers who treat every head like a signature.

Every appointment starts with a consultation and ends with a finish check. We cut with the grain of your lifestyle: how you style it, how it grows, how it should look three weeks from now.

The Ritual

How a MONO visit works

  1. 01

    Consult

    We read your hair, your growth pattern and your routine before a single blade moves.

  2. 02

    Craft

    Scissors, clippers and straight razor — the cut is built in layers, checked from every angle.

  3. 03

    Finish

    Hot towel, razor detailing, wash and style. You leave camera-ready, not salon-fresh.

  4. 04

    Maintain

    Product advice and a rebooking window so the cut looks deliberate for weeks — not days.
